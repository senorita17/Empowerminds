Mental Health Assessment

1. How often do you feel down, depressed, or hopeless?

2. How often do you have little interest or pleasure in doing things you used to enjoy?

3. How often have you been bothered by trouble falling asleep, staying asleep, or sleeping too much over the last two weeks?

4. How often have you been bothered by feeling tired or having little energy?

5. How often have you been bothered by poor appetite or overeating?

6. How often have you been bothered by feeling bad about yourself, or that you are a failure, or have let yourself or your family down?

7. How often have you been bothered by trouble concentrating on things, such as reading the newspaper or watching television?

8. How often have you been bothered by moving or speaking so slowly that other people could have noticed, or the opposite - being so fidgety or restless that you have been moving around a lot more than usual?

9. How often have you been bothered by not being able to stop or control worrying?

10. How often have you been bothered by having trouble relaxing?

11. How often have you been bothered by being so restless that it is hard to sit still?

12. How often have you been bothered by feeling afraid as if something awful might happen?

13. How often have you been bothered by becoming easily annoyed or irritable over the last two weeks?

14. Does your muscles get tense when you are worried or anxious?

15. Have you ever experienced an anxiety attack (suddenly feeling fear or panic)?
